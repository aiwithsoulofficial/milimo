The care your family would give, in the car they'd choose

Rideshare is a stranger in a hurry. A milimo senior transfer is a professional chauffeur who arrives early, walks with your loved one from the front door, stows the walker, takes the scenic route if there's time, and calls you when they arrive safely. It is transport the way it used to be done.

Door-through-door, never kerb-to-kerb

Assistance from the front door to the car and into the appointment or gathering at the other end. Help with bags and mobility aids. No rushing, ever.

Family peace of mind

Every kind of journey
