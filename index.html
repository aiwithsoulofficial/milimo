For parents - your safety checklist

milimo takes the safety of young passengers seriously. Here is what we have in place for every formal booking:

Working with Children - Blue Card

All chauffeurs servicing school formals hold current Blue Cards (Working with Children Check) as required under Queensland legislation.

Security Background Checks

Thorough security background screening is completed for all chauffeurs before they join the milimo team.

GPS Tracked Throughout the Night

All vehicles are GPS tracked from pickup to drop-off. You can call the office at any time to confirm your child's location.

Parent Contact on the Night

You will receive a direct mobile number for your chauffeur before departure. Direct contact throughout the evening.

Strict No-Alcohol Policy

milimo maintains a firm no-alcohol policy for all school formal bookings. Only soft drinks are provided. No exceptions.
